Sesame Beef

Strips of round steak and chopped scallions are marinated with soy sauce and garlic, then quickly stir fried with sesame seeds. Add a little sesame oil to the marinade if you like that flavor. Serve with rice.

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 1/2 pound round steak
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Directions

  1. 1. Mix soy sauce, sugar, oil, garlic, and onions in a large bowl. Set aside.
  2. 2. Cut steak into strips and add to bowl. Cover and refrigerate overnight, or at least 30 minutes.
  3. 3. Cook in wok or frying pan until brown, about 5 minutes. Add sesame seeds and cook for additional 2 minutes.
